Developer Student Club

Learn. Build. Teach.

Developer Student Club is a university-based community group for students interested in growing as a developer. By joining DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for the local community. Don't worry if haven't ever coded before, we have workshops for everyone.

Solution Challenge

The Google Solution Challenge is an annual competition open to university students under the mentorship umbrella of a DSC chapter. The solution must feature one or more Google technologies and must address a local problem faced by a business, organization, NGO, etc.
