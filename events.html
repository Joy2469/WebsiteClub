Study Jam

Qwiklabs

Study Jams are collective learning programs designed to give students practical experience in Google Cloud Tools. Like-minded developers come together to hone their skills and further their cloud knowledge through interactive classrooms and hands-on labs.
Upon completion of a study jam within a day, attendees earn badges certifying their skills which they can add to their LInkedIn profile.

The Google Solution Challenge is an annual completion open to university students under the mentorship umbrella of a DSC chapter. The solution must feature one or more Google technologies and must address a local problem faced by a business, organization, NGO, etc.
